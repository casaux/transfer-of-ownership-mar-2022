What is myCASA?

myCASA is an online portal where you can access services. To access myCASA services you will need:

  • A myCASA account
  • An aviation reference number
  • To verify your identity

Learn more about what services are available in myCASA.

What is Digital Identity?

A Digital Identity makes it easier to prove who you are online. It’s like the 100-point ID check, but on your smart device. Your Digital Identity makes accessing government services simpler, safer and more secure. Currently, myGovID is the app you will need to access government services using a Digital Identity.

myGovID is different to myGov. Your myGovID is your digital identity and unique to you. The myGovID app lets you prove who you are and securely sign in to participating government online services, such as myGov.

Standard identity strength using digital identity is required to access all services.
